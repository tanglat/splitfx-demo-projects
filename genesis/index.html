Frequently asked questions

Looking for answers to your frequently asked questions? Check out our FAQ's section below to find.

How do I create my first AI agent?

Simply start with a prompt describing what you want your agent to do. The builder automatically generates tasks and workflows, which you can then customize before launching.

Do I need coding skills to use the platform?

No coding is required. The platform is designed for both technical and non-technical users, allowing you to configure automation through an intuitive interface.

Can I integrate the agent with external tools?

Yes. You can connect your AI agent with popular tools, APIs, and data sources to automate workflows across your existing tech stack.

Is my data secure?

We prioritize security and privacy. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and you maintain full control over permissions and integrations.

Can I modify my agent after launching?

Absolutely. You can update tasks, adjust rules, and add new integrations at any time without interrupting your ongoing workflows.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is based on usage and feature access. You can start with a basic plan and upgrade as your automation needs grow.
